## Collaborating with teammates


### üßë‚Äçüíª Real-time editing

Comments are a great way to start a conversation about a page or blog post. They allow you to remark on content, add important information, ask questions, and generally drive collaboration and teamwork.

@mention a teammate to bring them into the conversation, and they can reply to and/or like comments.

## Organizing your content





### üå≥ Content tree

The content tree is where your content is organized within a space. Drag and drop to reorder or nest content, so you can more effectively share your work with others.


![](https://wac-cdn.atlassian.com/dam/jcr:667561bb-2450-4179-a333-b797a847fceb/getting_started_page_tree.gif)

- [ ] **After you publish, select `[` to expand or collapse the content tree.**

[More about the content tree](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/move-copy-and-hide-pages/)





### üì£ Space overview

To help your teammates find relevant content, curate your space overview with useful elements like calendars and content statuses, and include information about what is in your space.


![](https://wac-cdn.atlassian.com/dam/jcr:81fe1309-530c-48b6-9f26-7a75273e710e/getting_started_space_overview.png)

- [ ] **Use the `/info` panel element to highlight important information in your space overview.**

[More about space overview](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/set-up-your-space-homepage/)


### üåª Content types


![](https://wac-cdn.atlassian.com/misc-assets/webp-images/template-brainwriting-theme-white-v2.svg)**WHITEBOARD**


###### If your content still isn‚Äôt defined, whiteboards are a quick way to get messy ideas from many collaborators documented.


![](https://wac-cdn.atlassian.com/misc-assets/webp-images/template-blogpost.webp)**BLOG POST**


###### If your content that won‚Äôt change over time, create it as a blog post. The blog feed for each space displays the most recent posts first.
